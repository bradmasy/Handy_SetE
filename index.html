How Do We Do It?

At Handy we provide many key features that lets our audience make additional income with the help of our job postings. For all employers and employees alike, whether you are seeking help for your business or help for income, our company will do our best to help serve you.

Targetted Audience

Our target audience is for those that have been impacted by COVID-19. As we know, it has been a big struggle for all people. We aim to target those that especially have it bad by providing an easy tool to find jobs or help their business.
